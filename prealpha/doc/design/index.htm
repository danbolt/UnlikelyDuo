Plot/Blurb

Some Crazy Organization of Doom (SCOD) has taken the package and hidden it deep inside their nefarious base in the mountains! Guess what? The superiors need it back pronto! Who's to call, none other than the Unlikely Duo! None other than THEPILOT, ace pilot of his/her hovership, and THEAGENT, the crack whimsical brawler who can get get past any defence! Have you and your best friend take control and get the package back. But you'll need lots of smarts and bravery if you're to prove your might.
